About Map Lore Applications

Map

At the heart of the Thegye geopolitical roleplay is the Map, which displays all the geopolitical boundaries of the various player-nations. This map also outlines the boundaries of the Sacred Marintes Empire, displayed in light blue. You can find the key for each nation below the map image.

Map Claim

Along with your roleplay application, you will need to submit a map claim to be added to the map. Map claims should be submitted as an image or as text with specific instructions for placement, size, and (optionally) color. Claims may not overlap current nations. Click here to submit your claim.

Note that the map has the Sacred Marintes Empire in light blue. Players who have not received an exception to exist outside the Empire must claim land area within the Empire. The scale is 1 square kilometer for every pixel. Claims should correspond to the rank of the constituent state; kingdoms (rare) should be relatively large, free cities should be relatively small, and so on. Players are encouraged to ask questions or for help if they are unsure where to begin. Moderators may alter the size of your claim if it constitutes an area too large to be conducive to play.
