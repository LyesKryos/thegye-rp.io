Applications

In order to join Thegye's geopolitical roleplay, players will need to fill out and submit one of the two simple application forms to the roleplay moderators. Select whichever application fits your situation.

Imperial States

Most players should choose to exist within the empire. For states within the empire, fill out the following form:

  1. State Name: What is the full name of your imperial state?
  2. State Title: What title or equivalent does your ruler hold within the imperial peerage?
  3. Map Location: Include a screenshot or detailed description of your map placement.
  4. Brief Description: Include a short concept or description of what your polity is like and/or a narrative hook pertaining to it.

Non-Imperial/Extra-Imperial States

Some players may wish to exist partly or wholly outside of the bounds of the empire. As this falls outside of the roleplay's overarching narrative, players must justify why they wish to exist outside the empire and how they will remain in contact with the plot. For states outside the empire, fill out the following form:

  1. Nation Name: What is the full name of your nation?
  2. Map Location: Include a screenshot or detailed description of your map placement.
  3. Rationale: Why do you want to play outside of the empire?
  4. Independence: How has your polity remained independent from both the empire and external forces?
  5. Plot Interaction: How does your polity relate to and interact with the realms of the empire?
  6. Brief Description: Include a short concept or description of what your polity is like and/or a narrative hook pertaining to it.

Questions concerning applications should be forwarded to a roleplay moderator.
