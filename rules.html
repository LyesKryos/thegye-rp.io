Below find the rules that govern the Thegye Geopolitical Roleplay. These rules are enforced by the RP moderators and are non-negotiable. Participating in the Thegye Geopolitical Roleplay assumes an agreement to be bound by these rules.

Basic Rules

  1. Godmodding, power-playing, and meta-play are prohibited. Godmodding is defined as playing god with regard to a nation, characters, or assets, blatantly defying realism and refusing to participate in the roleplay in good faith. Power-playing is defined as attempting to control the actions or decisions of other players' nations, characters, or assets without permission. Meta-playing is using knowledge of the future or of out-of-character information for in-character advantage, especially when nations, characters, and assets would otherwise not be aware of that knowledge or information; this includes the use or knowledge of anachronistic technology, science, and other discoveries.
  2. Respect player soverignty. Unless entirely justified, do not make decisions or actions on behalf of other players. Instead, coordinate with other players and ensure that any action taken on behalf of other nations or with regard to other nations, characters, and assets is approved. Realistic cause-and-effect is not prohibited under this rule.
  3. Maintain a collaborative enviornment. A roleplay is nothing without collaboration, so seek to collaborate with other players as much as is reasonable. Players should seek to interact with other nations, characters, and assets as much as is reasonable; requests for interaction should rarely be ignored. This rule does not prohibit players from declining interaction if it is unwanted, but it does require courteous consideration and a timely response.
  4. Recognize the setting, overall plot, and worldbuilding. The setting maintains a late-Medieval time period, rare magic, and low-to-no fantasy elements. The plot sets a hook with the Holy Marinties Empire. Players should seek to engage and interact with the plot hook (see Plot Rules) and adhere to the worldbuilding. Likewise, players are encouraged to collaborate with the worldbuilding of players around them.
  5. Respect RP moderators and Discord rules. Roleplay should likewise take place in the appropriate channels. Roleplay elements are not considered canon unless posted in the appropriate Discord channel or stored as lore/worldbuilding. Interactions or plot developments that do not take place on the Discord server should be summarized within the Discord server to be considered canon.
